# Project Name (Working)

**Momentum AI – Your Personal Productivity Assistant**

## Vision

Momentum AI helps busy professionals, entrepreneurs, freelancers, and students organize their work and life using AI.

Instead of simply managing tasks, the app helps users decide **what to do, when to do it, and how to stay focused.**

## One feature that would make this stand out

Instead of users manually creating tasks, make **AI the primary experience**.

A user should be able to type or say:

> "I'm launching a new Shopify store next month."

The app should automatically generate:

* A complete project
* Milestones
* Tasks
* Deadlines
* Recommended schedule
* Estimated effort
* Weekly plan
* Reminders

That feels like having an executive assistant rather than using another task manager. This kind of experience is much more compelling and gives users a strong reason to subscribe to the premium plan.
